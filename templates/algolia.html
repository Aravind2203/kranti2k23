Algolia

Algolia is an event of competitive programming for individuals. There are 2 levels in total. The first level has 3 questions of medium difficulty and the second level has 2 questions of hard difficulty. Each level has a 45 minutes time limit. Winners will be selected according to the efficiency and correctness of the submitted code.
