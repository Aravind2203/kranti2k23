Shark Tank

Shark Tank is a competition for college students to pitch their business ideas to a panel of experts. The competition includes 2 rounds: Preliminary and Final. In the preliminary round, participants present their product/service and receive feedback. In the final round, shortlisted candidates pitch a hypothetical product(from the organizers) with a personal twist. The sharks choose to invest or not by showing a green or red flag after each pitch.
